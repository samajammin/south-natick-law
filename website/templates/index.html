Small Firm Attention. Big Firm Sophistication.

At McLaughlin, Richards, Biller & Schindel, LLP, we have the patience to listen to you. We take the time to understand your problems and the outcomes you hope to achieve. Our goal is to design and implement a plan to effectively and efficiently address your legal issues and achieve your goals. We believe that when the attorney and client work together as a team the sky is the limit.

Our attorneys possess over 100 years of combined experience in domestic relations, estate planning, real estate, probate and tax law. Whether your problem is complex or simple, we have the experience and expertise to find workable solutions at a reasonable cost.

Over the years, we have developed four pillars of practice that guide us in our representation of you:

  1. We adhere to the highest level of integrity and ethics, both professionally and personally.
  2. We provide highly sophisticated advice and planning while maintaining the warm, personal touch of a small firm.
  3. We are committed to breaking every problem down into its simplest elements so we can explain the issues and solutions in a simple, straight forward manner.
  4. We work hard to stay current in our areas of expertise. We attend conferences and seminars and study new developments and trends in the law.
In short, we utilize our combined knowledge and skill to obtain successful results for our clients both in and out of court.
